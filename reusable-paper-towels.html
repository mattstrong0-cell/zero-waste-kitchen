Our Full Review

The average American family spends $180 per year on paper towels and throws away 13 billion pounds of paper towels annually. Unpaper towels solve both problems with a single purchase. A set of 10-12 reusable cloths handles everything paper towels do — spills, wiping counters, drying hands, cleaning surfaces — and they last for years.

The best unpaper towels are made from organic cotton flannel or terry cloth. Flannel is softer and better for delicate surfaces; terry cloth is more absorbent and better for heavy-duty cleaning. Many people keep both on hand.

The roll-style versions are the easiest transition — they snap together and fit on a standard paper towel holder, so your kitchen routine doesn't change at all. When a cloth is used, unsnap it and toss it in a small basket; wash the whole batch at the end of the week.

🎯 Best For

Households looking to eliminate paper towels completely and save money long-term. Especially great for families with young children.
