Our Full Review

Conventional dishwasher tablets come in plastic tubs or individually wrapped in plastic film. Eco-friendly alternatives come in cardboard boxes or compostable wrappers — same cleaning power, zero plastic packaging.

The best eco dishwasher tablets use plant-derived enzymes and oxygen-based bleach instead of chlorine and phosphates. They're equally effective at removing grease, food residue, and hard water stains, and they're safe for septic systems — a key consideration for homeowners.

Brands like Seventh Generation, Ecover, and Dropps have proven track records. Dropps in particular offers a subscription model that reduces packaging further by shipping concentrated pods in minimal cardboard packaging.
