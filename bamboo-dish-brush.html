Our Full Review

Conventional plastic dish brushes are another overlooked source of plastic waste in the kitchen. The handle is typically polypropylene, the bristles are nylon, and neither component is recyclable. They end up in landfill after just a few months of use.

Bamboo dish brushes solve this with a handle that's fully compostable and bristles made from plant fibers (agave, sisal, or coconut) that biodegrade naturally. The cleaning performance is excellent — stiff enough to scrub pots and pans, gentle enough for non-stick surfaces.

The best bamboo brushes come with replaceable heads, so you can keep the handle for years and only replace the bristle head when it wears out. This dramatically reduces waste compared to replacing the entire brush.
